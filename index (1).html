Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Taste

Founded in 1968 by the Narayana family on the bustling streets of Jhansi, our chat bhandar began as a humble roadside stall driven by one simple belief — good food brings people together. Over five decades and three generations, that belief has never changed.

Our recipes remain untouched, our ingredients fresh, and our love for food as strong as ever. From the crispy crunch of Papdi Chaat to the soul-warming Matar Karela, every dish carries the flavour of tradition and the warmth of home.

Today, Narayana Chat Bhandar is not just a food shop — it's a part of Jhansi's identity, loved by children, parents, and grandparents alike.
